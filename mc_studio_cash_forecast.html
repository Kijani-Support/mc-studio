üìê Compensation Formula Reference

BD Team Commission:
Individual Commission = (Total MRR Closed by Individual / Total Team MRR) √ó 2% √ó Total MRR
Example: If BD Lead closes KES 425K out of KES 1.275M total MRR:
Commission = (425,000 / 1,275,000) √ó 0.02 √ó 1,275,000 = KES 8,500
QA Project Bonus:
Individual Bonus = Number of Projects Completed √ó KES 2,000
Example: QA Analyst completes 5 projects:
Bonus = 5 √ó 2,000 = KES 10,000
Monthly Profit Share (2%):
Step 1: Calculate Net Profit = Revenue - (Payroll + Bonuses + Operating Costs)
Step 2: Profit Share Pool = Net Profit √ó 2%
Step 3: Individual Share = Profit Share Pool √∑ Number of Staff
Example with KES 3M revenue:
Net Profit = 3,000,000 - 654,000 = 2,346,000
Profit Share Pool = 2,346,000 √ó 0.02 = 46,920
Per Person = 46,920 √∑ 9 = KES 5,213 each
