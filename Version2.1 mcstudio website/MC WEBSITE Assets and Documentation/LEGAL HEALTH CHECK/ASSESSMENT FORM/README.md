# LEGAL HEALTH CHECK - Assessment Form

This folder contains all form-related assets for the legal health check assessment.

## Content

- Form field definitions and labels
- Question text and options
- Form instructions and guidance
- Validation rules and error messages
- Form submission documentation
- Required field specifications
- Form templates or structure

## Subfolders

- **Images & Media/** - Form icons, instructional images
- **Text & Content/** - Form questions, field labels, instructions

## Best Practices

- Keep form questions clear and unambiguous
- Document field requirements
- Include helpful instructions
- Provide validation feedback
- Test form usability regularly
