# Individual Profiles

This folder contains individual startup company profiles.

## Content

- Startup company descriptions
- Founder and leadership information
- Company mission and vision
- Product and service descriptions
- Key metrics and growth numbers
- Funding and investment information
- Industry and sector information
- Social media and contact links
- Achievements and milestones

## Subfolders

- **Images & Media/** - Startup logos, founder photos, product screenshots, office images
- **Text & Content/** - Company bios, founder bios, company descriptions, achievement summaries

## Best Practices

- Keep startup information current
- Include high-quality photos and logos
- Highlight key achievements
- Include founder bios
- Document metrics and growth
- Include social media links
