# Success Stories

This folder contains success stories and achievement highlights from portfolio startups.

## Content

- Success story narratives and case studies
- Startup achievements and milestones
- Growth metrics and results
- Founder testimonials and quotes
- Challenges overcome and lessons learned
- Product/service success highlights
- Customer impact stories
- Media mentions and press coverage
- Financial achievements (funding, revenue, exits)

## Subfolders

- **Images & Media/** - Startup photos, achievement graphics, celebration images, media coverage images
- **Text & Content/** - Success story narratives, achievement summaries, testimonial quotes

## Best Practices

- Tell compelling success stories
- Include specific metrics and results
- Feature founder quotes and insights
- Document challenges and solutions
- Highlight customer impact
- Include media coverage and recognition
- Keep stories updated and current
