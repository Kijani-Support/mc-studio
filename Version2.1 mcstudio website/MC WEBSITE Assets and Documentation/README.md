# MC Studio Website Documentation & Assets

## Overview
This directory contains all the assets, documentation, and content structure for the MC Studio website. It is organized by page sections and includes both visual media and textual content for each website component.

---

## Directory Structure

### 1. **HOME**
The landing/homepage section of the website.
- **Images & Media**: Hero images, banners, and multimedia assets
- **Text and Content**: Homepage copy, headlines, and descriptions

---

### 2. **ABOUT US**
Company information and organizational details.
- **Images & Media**: Team photos, company imagery, brand assets
- **Text & Content**: Company mission, vision, team bios, company history

---

### 3. **SERVICES**
Information about services offered by MC Studio.

#### Sub-sections:
- **SERVICE OFFERING**: Main service descriptions and value propositions
  - Images & Media
  - Text & Content
  
- **ADVISORY**: Consulting and advisory services
  
- **AIKYA LEGAL**: Legal services and compliance offerings
  
- **PRICING**: Service pricing models and packages
  
- **QA SERVICES**: Quality assurance service details
  
- **SECTOR-SPECIFIC**: Industry-specific service solutions

---

### 4. **CONTACT**
Contact and communication information.
- **Images & Media**: Contact form visuals, location maps, office images
- **Text & Content**: Contact information, support details, inquiry forms

---

### 5. **FAQ**
Frequently Asked Questions section.
- **Images & Media**: Infographics, instructional images
- **Text & Content**: Question-answer pairs, helpful guidance

---

### 6. **PROJECTS**
Portfolio and project showcase.

#### Sub-sections:
- **PROJECT DETAILS**: Individual project information and case studies
- **PROJECT PORTFOLIO**: Complete project listings and showcases

---

### 7. **PARTNERS**
Partnership and collaboration information.

#### Sub-sections:
- **IBM PARTNERSHIP OVERVIEW**: IBM partnership details and benefits
- **INVESTOR PORTAL**: Investor relations and opportunities
- **OW ADVOCATES**: Advocacy and ambassador program information
- **PARTNER ECOSYSTEM**: Partner network and collaboration framework
- **PARTNERSHIP APP**: Partner application and portal details

---

### 8. **STARTUPS**
Startup program and ecosystem.

#### Sub-sections:
- **Individual Profiles**: Startup founder/member profiles
- **Join Ecosystem**: Onboarding and application information
- **Portfolio Directory**: Startup portfolio and listings
- **Success-Stories**: Case studies and success narratives

---

### 9. **IBM PRODUCTS**
IBM product offerings and solutions.

#### Sub-sections:
- **AI & DATA SOLUTION**: AI and Data analytics offerings
- **AUTOMATION & INTEGRATION**: Automation and system integration solutions
- **CLOUD & INFRASTRUCTURE**: Cloud services and infrastructure
- **IBM AUTHORIZATION PRODUCT (NEW)**: New authorization products
- **IBM FOR STARTUPS**: Special startup programs
- **IBM RESOURCE**: Resource library and documentation
- **IMPLEMENTATION & SUPPORT**: Implementation and technical support
- **INDUSTRY SOLUTIONS**: Vertical/industry-specific solutions
- **REQUEST QUOTE**: Quote request forms and pricing
- **SECURITY SOLUTIONS**: Security and compliance products
- **SOFTWARE LICENSING**: Licensing information and options

---

### 10. **SUPPORT CENTER**
Customer support and help resources.
- **Images & Media**: Support visuals, help documentation images
- **Text AND CONTENT**: Support documentation, FAQs, troubleshooting guides

---

### 11. **LEGAL HEALTH CHECK**
Legal compliance and assessment tools.

#### Sub-sections:
- **ASSESSMENT FORM**: Legal assessment questionnaire
- **FAQ**: Legal compliance frequently asked questions
- **HEALTH ASSESSMENT**: Compliance health check details
- **RESULTS PAGE**: Assessment results and recommendations
- **STARTUP DASHBOARD (AUTH)**: Authenticated dashboard for startups

---

## File Organization

Each major section follows this structure:
```
SECTION_NAME/
├── IMAGES & MEDIA/
│   └── (Visual assets, photos, icons, videos)
├── TEXT & CONTENT/
│   └── (Copy, descriptions, metadata)
└── Additional subsections (if applicable)
```

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Content Updates**: Modify text content in the respective `TEXT & CONTENT` folders
2. **Image Management**: Update visual assets in the `IMAGES & MEDIA` folders
3. **Subsection Content**: Follow the hierarchical structure when organizing new content
4. **Naming Conventions**: See the main repository `file_naming_system.txt` for naming standards
