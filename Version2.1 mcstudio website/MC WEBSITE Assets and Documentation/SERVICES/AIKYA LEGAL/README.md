# AIKYA LEGAL

This folder contains assets and documentation for Aikya Legal services.

## Content

- Legal service descriptions and offerings
- Practice areas and expertise
- Attorney profiles and backgrounds
- Legal services process and timeline
- Pricing structure
- Case studies and success stories
- Certifications and credentials
- Contact information for legal services

## Subfolders

- **Images & Media/** - Attorney photos, service diagrams, credentials images
- **Text & Content/** - Service descriptions, attorney bios, case studies

## Best Practices

- Highlight attorney qualifications
- Document service areas clearly
- Include client testimonials
- Show practice area expertise
- Provide transparent pricing
- Keep certifications current
