# ADVISORY

This folder contains assets and documentation for advisory services.

## Content

- Advisory service descriptions
- Consulting expertise areas
- Advisory process and methodology
- Consultant profiles and backgrounds
- Case studies and success stories
- Pricing and engagement models
- Industry expertise
- Contact information for advisory services

- **Text & Content/** - Service descriptions, consultant bios, case studies

- Highlight consultant expertise
- Document service methodologies
- Include client success stories
- Show industry experience
- Provide clear pricing information
- Include testimonials
