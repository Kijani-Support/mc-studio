# PRICING

This folder contains service pricing and cost information.

## Content

- Service pricing models
- Price per service or package
- Volume and enterprise discounts
- Special offers and promotions
- Comparison tools
- Payment options and terms
- Contract information
- ROI calculations

## Subfolders

- **Images & Media/** - Pricing comparison charts, promotional graphics, package icons
- **Text & Content/** - Pricing details, payment terms, discount information

## Best Practices

- Keep pricing current and accurate
- Provide clear pricing structure
- Document discount policies
- Include payment options
- Provide ROI information
- Include special offer details
