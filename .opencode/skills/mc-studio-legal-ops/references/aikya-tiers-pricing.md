# Aikya Legal Tiers & Pricing

## Overview

Three tiers — **HUSTLE → GROW → LEAD** — determined by headcount, funding stage, and annual revenue bands (not just company age). Every engagement starts with a **Legal Health Check (KSh 25,000)**, which routes the startup into the appropriate tier.

## Tier Eligibility

| Criterion | HUSTLE | GROW | LEAD |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Annual Revenue (KES)** | < 20,000,000 | ≥ 20,000,000 | ≥ 500,000,000 |
| **Headcount** | 1–10 | 11–50 | 51+ |
| **Funding Stage** | Pre-seed / Bootstrapped | Seed / Series A | Series B+ / Growth |
| **LHC Score Risk Level** | Any | Moderate–Low (≥50) | Low (≥75) |
| **Typical Sector Signal** | Early-stage, single founder | Licensed fintech, revenue-generating | Multi-country, regulated industries |

> **Note:** These are guidelines, not hard gates. A fintech startup with modest headcount but CBK licensing exposure may be routed to GROW even below the revenue band.

## Fee Formula (per Engagement)

```
Total_Fee_KES = Base_Fee_KES × Duration_Months + Lotus_Txn_Fee_KES
Deposit_Required_KES = Total_Fee_KES × 0.5 + 250  (50% + flat KES 250 Lotus fee)
Balance_Due_KES = Total_Fee_KES − Deposit_Paid_KES
```

- **Base_Fee_KES** = the tier's monthly retainer
- **Lotus_Txn_Fee_KES** = KES 250 per Lotus transaction (flat)
- **Duration_Months** = the engagement term
